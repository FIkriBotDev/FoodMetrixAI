<h1 align="center">FoodMetrix AI</h1>

###

<h2 align="left">🎯 About</h2>

###

<p align="left">FoodMetrix AI is an AI-powered food analysis platform that scans food images and generates detailed nutritional insights in seconds.<br><br>Using the pollinations.ai API for vision and reasoning capabilities, FoodMetrix AI analyzes uploaded or captured food images and provides:<br><br>- Health score evaluation<br>- Estimated calories per serving<br>- Macronutrient breakdown (protein, carbs, fat)<br>- Sugar and sodium estimation<br>- Ingredient analysis<br>- Healthier alternatives<br>- Consumption frequency recommendations<br>- Visual macro composition charts<br><br>The app leverages pollinations.ai's multimodal capabilities to interpret food images and generate structured nutritional intelligence in a clean and user-friendly dashboard.<br><br>FoodMetrix AI aims to make nutrition awareness accessible through AI-powered food scanning.</p>
